Values

My practice is informed by these values:

  • Relationality – The corner-stone. Once relationship is established we can begin the journey.
  • Empowering & person-centered – Here to support your journey. I trust that you have the knowledge within you and simply need support to chart your own course.
  • Strengths-based – Through our challenges, we grow.
  • Narrative – Stories connect us and carry a resonance that embed our learnings.
  • Culturally responsive – Our unique and multifaceted cultural lens provides important context for how we view and navigate the world.

How I can support you

My style of coaching brings together a range of tools to support you to learn and grow through life’s challenges, and supervision, training and consultation are available to support both individual practice and organisational needs.

Supervision

Supervision is a form of supported reflective practice that allows you to consider and process issues in your work life; both the successes and the challenges. My style of supervision utilises strengths-based, narrative and reflective approaches. I use a blend of motivational interviewing techniques, Socratic questioning and reflective learning cycles to support your process of insight and knowledge gaining. I am a registered social worker, who also supervises people from other professional backgrounds.

Coaching

My style of coaching brings together a range of tools to support you to learn and grow through life’s challenges. The tools and frameworks include CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy), Motivational Interviewing, holistic well-being models (including Te Whare Tapa Wha), Socratic questioning, strengths-based and person-centered approaches, Whānau Ora principles, reflective learning processes, and narrative, metaphor and story-telling.

Training & Consultation

Standard training is offered on LGBTTQIA+ / gender diversity, elder abuse, mental health and AOD, and mental wellbeing, with bespoke training to meet organisational needs available on request. Consultation work can take many forms; I have experience in facilitating strategic and team processes and in reviewing programmes and organisational issues.
