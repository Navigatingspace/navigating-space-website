How I can support you

Supervision, coaching, consultation and training are available for individuals and organisations.

Supervision

Supporting reflective practice using strengths-based, narrative and reflective approaches. Promoting your growth and wellbeing in your work, no matter your profession or career stage.

Coaching

Coaching informed by CBT, motivational interviewing, Te Whare Tapa Wha, and meaning-making. No matter the size of your goal we can invest together in supporting you to take meaningful steps towards it..

Training & Consultation

Training available on LGBTTQIA+ topics, elder abuse, mental health/AOD, wellbeing, and bespoke organisational needs. Experience serving on and contracting to NGO boards, including facilitating strategic direction and other workshops.
